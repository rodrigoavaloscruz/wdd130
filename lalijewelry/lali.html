Hello, I'm Sandra Cruz Jaimes

I am 58 years old, born in Mexico City, Mexico. The pearls have been a fantasy for me, a dream, a smile, and a sparkle in my eyes. I love perfect round white pearls. In 2010 I bought different types of pearls to enjoy their texture and contemplate their colors and shine. In that same year, I learned everything about the process of making handmade necklaces and bracelets. My sister Norma was my teacher. At first, I only made round pearl necklaces because I didn't like to mix any other type of material with the pearls. Over time, I added other materials to my designs, such as crystals, metals, and stones. I believe that handmade necklaces and bracelets can highlight the beauty of people. From 2010 to date, I still make necklaces and bracelets with the same passion as the first time.
