Content

Home page

The website will be easy to attract those people, who are looking for a handmade necklace and bracelet made in Mexico for a great talent female artist, to our website, engage our visitors, and work towards getting the visitors to take action on our website. The website will organize the information in four sections in the main navigation. The website will provide clear paths for navigation. The following questions will be answered on this website: Who is the artist who made the handmade necklaces, bracelets, and earrings? What are the materials that are used on the items? What is the meaning of each item made by the artist? Where I can buy the items? The website will have a section where it will inform who is the artist, the company, and our operations. This section will be communicated the story of Lali Jewelry and why we started it. Furthermore, this section will explain how our products are made and where we get the materials. Also, this section will feature the founders and the team members of the Lili Jewelry team. The visitors will have the contact us section where they can leave feedback, request items, or ask for more information about our services. The visitors will need to leave their names and email to contact them back. This section will redirect to an external email. This section will help to build the LALI JEWELRY brand website as it will allow our future visitors to contact us. This section also will help us to improve customer service. The website will inform our visitors what our business is about, what we stand for, and how our visitors can benefit from what we have to offer in the handmade jewelry industry.

Images for the Home page

Necklaces

It will display only the handmade necklaces with information about each one. Each item will have a link to click where they will be directed to our external shopping page to buy it. Every item will have a link where the visitors can request to personalize the items at an extra price. This section will facilitate the buying and selling process. The idea right now for this website is only informative as a catalog. However, in the future, the website will have its direct buy section without visiting an external page to buy the items.
